📘 About StudyMate AI

StudyMate AI is a powerful AI-based study assistant designed to help students learn faster and smarter.

It allows users to upload study materials and instantly generate quizzes, summaries, and mind maps using advanced artificial intelligence.

🎯 Our Mission

Our goal is to make learning more efficient, interactive, and accessible for every student.

✨ What You Can Do

  • Generate quizzes from your notes
  • Create AI-powered summaries
  • Visualize concepts with mind maps
  • Use AI tutor for better understanding
